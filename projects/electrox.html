ElectroX

Multi-Vendor E-commerce Platform

ElectroX is a comprehensive multi-vendor e-commerce web application specifically designed for electronic devices and accessories. The platform provides an intuitive online marketplace where users can browse a wide array of products listed by various sellers.

Main Objectives

  • Develop a multi-vendor e-commerce web application for electronic devices and accessories
  • Provide intuitive online platform for buyers to browse products from various sellers
  • Enable secure cart management and payment processing
  • Allow sellers to create accounts, list products, and manage inventory
  • Implement comprehensive order tracking and management system

How ElectroX Works

ElectroX operates as a marketplace where buyers can browse products, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, and make secure payments. Meanwhile, sellers can create accounts to list products, manage inventory, and track orders through dedicated vendor dashboards.
